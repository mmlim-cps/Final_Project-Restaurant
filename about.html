Googoo's Table was started by a family whose passion is food. Growing up in Cambodia, we watched as our parents served fresh noodle soups and hand roasted coffee from a roadside stall. The soup was simmered for hours, and had a flavor that was both Chinese and Southeast Asian. Family meals at home reflected this melting pot and similarly featured a diverse range of flavors - garlicky Chinese stir fries, earthy Cambodian stew, and fragrant Vietnamese noodles. This was our childhood memory of food. Our family's table then is a reflection of our unique story of being Chinese from Southeast Asia. We have been dedicated to serving our customers fresh and healthy food since we started our first restaurant here in 1986. As our family's business passes to the next generation, we hope to share with you more of these flavors that remind us of home. In that sense, Googoo's Table honors the tradition and the legacy that was started long ago at the noodle stall.
